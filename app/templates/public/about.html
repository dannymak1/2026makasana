Who We Are

Makasana Consultancy is a professional, full-service advisory firm dedicated to simplifying the complexities of doing business in Kenya. We specialize in providing synchronized solutions that span legal compliance, advanced tax management, government tendering, and technical project support.

We serve as a strategic partner to SMEs and organizations, helping establish robust administrative foundations and unlock sustainable growth opportunities in both the public and private sectors.
