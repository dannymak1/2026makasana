Our Services

Makasana Consultancy provides integrated advisory and execution support across business compliance, tax management, procurement readiness, and technical operations.

Business Foundation & Compliance

We support business registration and legal compliance through Limited Company registration, CR12 processing, business name registration, conversion to limited company, partnership deed preparation, annual returns, county permits, eCitizen linkage, and other statutory requirements.

Tax & Financial Management

Our tax support covers VAT filing, income tax filing, corporation tax, balance of tax, installment tax, rental income tax, company PIN application, tax compliance certification, ETR setup, and international tax advisory including import and export tax management.

Tendering & Procurement

We help organizations pursue public sector opportunities through AGPO certificate applications, prequalification document preparation, IFMIS registration, IFMIS tutorials, tender bidding support, and site visit certification preparation.

Technical & Operational Support

Our technical support spans architectural works, civil engineering services, advanced drafting with tools such as AutoCAD and Revit, BOQ preparation, cost analysis, project registration, site supervision, quality control, and CDC AC advice.
